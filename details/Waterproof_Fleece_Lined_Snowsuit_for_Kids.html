Winter-Ready Protection for Your Little One

This waterproof snowsuit is built to keep your child cozy and safe during the coldest days. Its 100% waterproof fabric with taped seams gives reliable protection against snow, rain, and wind, while the 3000g/m² breathable layer ensures they stay dry and comfortable. A fleece-lined interior adds an extra layer of warmth and insulation, making it ideal for outdoor play in the snow or for daily wear in chilly weather.

Comfort Meets Safety

The adjustable fit design ensures a snug and secure fit for growing bodies, with an inner toggle that allows for easy layering beneath. Elasticized wrist and ankle cuffs prevent water from entering, keeping your child dry and protected. The adjustable hood and reflective stripes add functionality and style, helping them stay visible and safe during winter adventures.
